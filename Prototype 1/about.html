ABOUT US

--The Weeknd is a Canadian singer, songwriter, and record producer whose real name is Abel Makkonen Tesfaye. He first gained widespread recognition with his mixtapes in the early 2010s and has since become one of the most successful recording artists in the world, with hits like "Blinding Lights," "Can't Feel My Face," and "Starboy."
In terms of mission and values, The Weeknd has been open about using his platform to raise awareness about social and political issues, particularly those affecting Black communities. He has also been vocal about his support for various social justice causes, including the Black Lives Matter movement.
The Weeknd is also committed to promoting diversity, inclusion, and sustainability through his music and his annual festival, "The Weeknd Presents: After Hours." The festival, which takes place in Las Vegas, features a diverse lineup of performers from different genres and backgrounds, and has made a conscious effort to include more female and underrepresented artists.
In addition, the festival has taken steps to promote sustainability, including using biodegradable products and implementing recycling programs. The Weeknd himself has also made efforts to reduce his environmental impact by advocating for sustainable practices and supporting initiatives that address climate change.
